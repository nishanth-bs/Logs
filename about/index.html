About Me

👋 hi! I’m Nishanth, and I’ve been building iOS apps for over 6.5 years.

I’m a Mobile Engineer 3 at slice Small Finance Bank — and I’ve been part of the ride since the early team days.

we’ve gone through a lot of pivots — from gift cards → EMI credit cards → UPI → a licensed bank. I’ve helped ship through every one of them.

these days, I lead engineering across:

  • payments (UPI, cards)
  • mobile infra
  • app performance at scale

our goal? build the most loved bank in India. and I’m proud to be helping shape it from the ground up.

before Slice, I was at Nuclei, where I built SDKs used by major banks across India and the Middle East. some things I worked on:

  • a cab aggregator SDK (live in ICICI, YONO SBI, etc.)
  • multi-SDK integration infra
  • rebuilt Federal Bank’s iOS native app from scratch

sometimes I also work with early-stage startups and founders — helping them get their v1s right in edtech, e-commerce, and finance.

🛠️ What I care about?#

I care deeply about:

  • clean, scalable mobile architecture
  • fast, stable releases
  • and sharing what I’ve learned the hard way

this blog is where I write about all of that. 🚀

this site is where I share the stuff I wish someone had shown me when I was starting out.
